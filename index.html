Process

From raw syllabus to
a structured study path.

No manual setup. Drop a text file — Lumina extracts every chapter, builds a milestone map, and sets your pace to your exam date.

01

Upload your syllabus

Drop a plain .txt file of your syllabus or chapter list. Lumina reads every heading, subheading, and topic — no formatting required on your end.

02

Set your exam date

Tell Lumina when your exam is. It builds a day-by-day milestone sequence that paces your study without overwhelming you in the final week.

03

Study with AI, topic by topic

Every milestone unlocks four modes — Explain, Summary, Assessment, Concept Graph — each generated from your actual syllabus, not generic content.

04

Track, streak, complete

Mark milestones done, build daily streaks, and watch your progress across every subject in one clean dashboard. Nothing hidden, nothing gamified.
